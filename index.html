Our story

A little place with a lot of glow.

Foodie Glow started as a single stovetop and a stubborn belief that comfort food deserves the same care as fine dining. Today we're a full kitchen serving hearty plates, slow-braised classics, and desserts finished tableside — every dish plated to catch the light of our dining room and, hopefully, yours.
